About Philippine Statistiko

Philippine Statistiko provides real-time population statistics and demographic data for the Philippines. Our mission is to make accurate demographic information accessible to everyone—researchers, students, policymakers, and concerned citizens.

This platform uses data-driven projections to visualize population changes as they happen, including:

  • Live population counter
  • Birth and death rates
  • Migration patterns
  • Demographic trends

Whether you're conducting research, creating policy, or simply curious about population dynamics in the Philippines, our tool provides up-to-date information in an easy-to-understand format.

Data Sources & Methodology

Primary Data Sources

All statistics displayed on Philippine Statistiko are based on official data from:

  • United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs: Population Division
    • World Population Prospects 2024 Revision
    • International Migration Report 2023
  • Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA)
    • Census of Population and Housing
    • Civil Registration and Vital Statistics

Calculation Methodology

Our real-time counters use the following approach:

  1. Starting with base population figures for January 1, 2025
  2. Applying annual rates for births, deaths, and migration
  3. Distributing these events evenly throughout the year
  4. Adjusting for known seasonal variations where data is available

Key Assumptions for 2025

  • Starting Population:
    The base population for the beginning of 2025 is 117,734,094, as estimated on January 1, 2025.
  • Population Growth:
    The Philippines is projected to grow by 1,861,376 people throughout the year, reaching 119,595,470 by January 1, 2026.
  • Natural Increase:
    • Estimated live births: 2,032,090 more than deaths in 2025.
    • Average daily births: 7,725
    • Average daily deaths: 2,158
  • Net Migration:
    • Estimated net migration: -170,714 for the year.
    • Average daily emigration: -468

Note: All figures represent projections based on historical trends and demographic models. Actual population counts may vary.
